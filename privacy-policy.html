Privacy Policy

Last updated: Oct 30, 2025

At KD NexCore Technologies ("we", "us", "our"), we are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you interact with our website and when we provide KD NexCore implementation, customization, hosting, and support services.

Information We Collect

  • Contact data: name, email, phone, company.
  • Project details: business requirements and workflows provided for KD NexCore projects.
  • Usage data: pages visited, referrer, device/browser information.
  • Technical data: logs generated during support and hosting (where applicable).

How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to inquiries and provide proposals.
  • To deliver KD NexCore implementation, customization, integrations, hosting, and support.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share information with trusted service providers (e.g., cloud hosting, Formspree for contact forms) under confidentiality and data processing agreements, and only as necessary to deliver our services.

Given our presence in Pakistan, UAE, and Liberia, your information may be processed across these locations subject to appropriate safeguards.

Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect your data. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.
